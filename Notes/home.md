# 🚀 RatVault Developer Dashboard

Welcome to **RatVault**, your personal multi-LLM-powered developer knowledge vault. This is where development knowledge lives—organized, searchable, and always at your fingertips.

Whether you're building with Python, scripting with Bash, mastering development tools, or diving into AI/ML concepts, RatVault has you covered.

---

## 💬 Quick Start

### Ask Anything
Use the **chat panel** (bottom right) to ask questions about any topic in the vault. Your questions are answered by cutting-edge LLMs with full context from our knowledge base.

**Examples:**
- "How do I handle async/await in Python?"
- "What's the best way to optimize Bash scripts?"
- "Explain transformer architectures"
- "How do I set up a development environment on macOS?"

## 🎯 How to Use RatVault

### 1. **Search**
Use the search function to find specific topics, commands, or concepts instantly.

### 2. **Chat**
Ask natural language questions via the chat panel. The AI will pull relevant information from the vault and answer comprehensively.

### 3. **Browse**
Click on any topic above to explore its contents. Start with sections that match your current learning goals.

### 4. **Link Navigation**
Each document includes internal links to related topics. Follow these to build deeper understanding.

### 5. **Contribute**
Found something useful? See a gap in our knowledge base? [Contribute](#contributing) your insights to make RatVault better for everyone.

## 💡 Pro Tips

**Maximize your RatVault experience:**

- **Chain your searches** — Search for a topic, then use chat to ask deeper questions about what you found

## ℹ️ About RatVault

**RatVault** is a next-generation developer knowledge vault designed for:

- **Speed** — Find answers in seconds, not minutes
- **Accuracy** — Curated, high-quality content (not web search spam)
- **Context** — AI understands relationships between concepts
- **Accessibility** — Works offline, searchable, structured
- **Growth** — Community-driven, continuously expanding knowledge base

RatVault harnesses multiple LLMs to provide intelligent, context-aware answers to your technical questions. Unlike generic search or documentation, RatVault understands the relationships between concepts and adapts answers to your specific context.
